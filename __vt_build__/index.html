About Us

Established in 2013, Alghurabi Manpower Services LLC (AMS) has swiftly grown into a leading manpower company, catering to the recruitment needs of clients across the Gulf region, Europe, and the African continent. With a strong commitment to excellence and a customer-centric approach, AMS has earned a reputation for delivering top-notch recruitment solutions tailored to the specific needs of its diverse clientele.

Comprehensive Expertise:

AMS possesses extensive expertise in the recruitment field, covering a wide range of industries and job categories. From engineering and construction to hospitality, healthcare, IT, and beyond, AMS has the knowledge and experience to source top talent across various sectors.

Proactive Talent Sourcing

AMS employs proactive talent sourcing strategies to identify and engage top talent before they become actively seeking employment. Through targeted outreach, networking events, and talent mapping exercises, AMS stays ahead of the curve in identifying potential candidates for its clients.

Branch Network:

AMS has established a robust network of branches across key locations in the Gulf region and the African continent to better serve its clients and candidates. With strategically located offices in UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Kurdistan, and select African countries, AMS ensures local presence and personalized service delivery.

Here's an outline of nine major projects that have taken place in the Gulf countries over the last 10 years, requiring significant manpower:

Dubai Expo 2020 (now held in 2021 due to COVID-19)

A world exposition held in Dubai, UAE, showcasing innovation

Human Resource Provided:

Required a large workforce for construction, event management, hospitality, security, transportation, and various support services.

King Abdullah Economic City (KAEC), Saudi Arabia

A mega-development project initiated by the Saudi government to diversify the economy and create jobs.

Human Resource Provided:

Involved construction, infrastructure development, manufacturing, logistics, and service industries, requiring a significant workforce.

Qatar 2022 FIFA World Cup

Qatar hosted the FIFA World Cup in 2022, requiring extensive infrastructure development, stadium construction, transportation systems, and hospitality services.

Human Resource Provided:

Demand for skilled and unskilled labor across various sectors, including construction, engineering, hospitality, transportation, and event management.

Yas Island Development, Abu Dhabi, UAE

A major leisure and entertainment destination in Abu Dhabi, featuring theme parks, hotels, malls, and residential communities.

Human Resource Provided:

Large-scale construction projects, hospitality services, retail operations, and event management, requiring a diverse workforce.

Doha Metro Project, Qatar

A multi-billion-dollar project to develop an integrated metro system in Doha, Qatar, ahead of the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Human Resource Provided:

Required skilled labor for tunneling, construction, engineering, project management, and operations across multiple phases of development.

Red Sea Project, Saudi Arabia

A tourism and hospitality project along the Red Sea coast of Saudi Arabia, aimed at developing luxury resorts, cultural attractions, and nature reserves.

Human Resource Provided:

Involved construction, landscaping, hospitality, environmental conservation, and tourism services, requiring a large workforce.

Abu Dhabi Vision 2030

Abu Dhabi's long-term economic development plan aimed at diversifying the economy, promoting innovation, and creating sustainable growth.

Human Resource Provided:

Various initiatives across sectors such as renewable energy, technology, healthcare, education, and infrastructure development, requiring skilled professionals and laborers.

Neom Project, Saudi Arabia

A futuristic city development project in Saudi Arabia, aiming to create a sustainable and technologically advanced urban center.

Human Resource Provided:

Involved infrastructure projects, healthcare initiatives, education reforms, and investment in various sectors, creating job opportunities across different industries.

Kuwait National Development Plan (New Kuwait 2035)

Kuwait's strategic development plan aimed at diversifying the economy, improving infrastructure, and enhancing public services.

Human Resource Provided:
