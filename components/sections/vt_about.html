About Us

Established in 2013, Alghurabi Manpower Services LLC (AMS) has swiftly grown into a leading manpower company, catering to the recruitment needs of clients across the Gulf region, Europe, and the African continent. With a strong commitment to excellence and a customer-centric approach, AMS has earned a reputation for delivering top-notch recruitment solutions tailored to the specific needs of its diverse clientele.

Comprehensive Expertise:

AMS possesses extensive expertise in the recruitment field, covering a wide range of industries and job categories. From engineering and construction to hospitality, healthcare, IT, and beyond, AMS has the knowledge and experience to source top talent across various sectors.

Proactive Talent Sourcing

AMS employs proactive talent sourcing strategies to identify and engage top talent before they become actively seeking employment. Through targeted outreach, networking events, and talent mapping exercises, AMS stays ahead of the curve in identifying potential candidates for its clients.

Branch Network:

AMS has established a robust network of branches across key locations in the Gulf region and the African continent to better serve its clients and candidates. With strategically located offices in UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Kurdistan, and select African countries, AMS ensures local presence and personalized service delivery.
